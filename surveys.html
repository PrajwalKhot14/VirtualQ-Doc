Surveys:

We conducted a survey to get an insight about the waiting room experience of users. To do this we created a Google Form consisting of eight questions about how users feel about waiting rooms. We asked the following questions in the Survey:

  1. Would it help you if you were informed about wait times in realtime?
  2. What slot do you prefer during a walk-in appointment?
  3. How would you utilize your wait time?
  4. Have you ever utilized your wait time to run errands?
  5. Have you ever missed your appointment because of running your errands?
  6. Due to an uncertain wait time, have you ever left the waiting room?
  7. What errands would you like to run during your wait time?
  8. If you are accompanying/being accompanied by someone for your appointment, how would they want to utilize their time?
