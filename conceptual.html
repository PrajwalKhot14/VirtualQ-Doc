VirtualQ focuses on eliminating the physical waiting rooms by giving patients the ability to join a virtual waiting room. Patients can wait for medical care from the comfort of their own space around the hospital – or get on with their daily lives and get things done – instead of waiting in a waiting room of a hospital. Timely updates notify patients when it’s their turn, with the option to move down in line if they need a little extra time. In addition, with Virtual Waiting Room, if the patient finds the wait time to be longer than expected they can leave the virtual waiting room .
